About Me

My name is Summer Westbrook and I’m a senior at App State studying Architecture and Construction Management. I’m especially interested in sustainable building practices and designing spaces that integrate seamlessly with the built environment. I’m passionate about incorporating user experience into my designs and making a space that feels both functional and innovative.

I believe all of the best projects come from collaboration and building on one another’s strengths. I love designing spaces that not only look good, but also enhance the lives of the people who will use them most. I’m excited to see what impact I’ll be able to make further down my career.
